How to Survive a Compliance Review

Understanding the Process

Compliance reviews are one of the most challenging parts of working as as Safety Director for a national trucking company. It is important to understand the reason your company has been selected for the review. The main reason a trucking company is selected for compliance review is due to high CSA scores and or a series of serious trucking safety events.

Most Common Safety Events to Trigger a Compliance Review

  • CSA Crash percentage over 65
  • CSA Unsafe driving percentage over 65
  • Fatility accident
  • Any combination of the above listed items
